About Me

Hi, I’m Murray Ridgway, a passionate web developer with a background in computing and game programming. I completed a Level 3 BTEC in Computing and studied two years of a Games Programming degree, which gave me hands-on experience with programming fundamentals, problem-solving, and software development principles.

I’ve always been fascinated by how the technology we use every day works — from websites and video games to computers in general. This curiosity is what first drew me to computing and programming. I enjoy understanding how things are built from the ground up and applying that knowledge to create functional and engaging digital experiences.

During my studies, I built projects using C++, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which helped me gain practical skills and understand good coding practices. These experiences form the foundation of my approach to web development today.

I’m particularly passionate about front-end development, creating responsive and visually engaging user experiences. I also enjoy learning about new technologies like React and exploring ways to optimize web performance and accessibility.

Feel free to explore my projects to see examples of my work, from small interactive websites to more complex development challenges.
